~ About us ~

Location

Thanks for stopping by. We are loacted at the east corner in Russel square park, serving fresh, healthy and handmade bubble tea. It only takes a few minutes to browse through our website and check out our menu. We hope you'll soon join us for a superb fresh bubble tea experience.

Story about us

Our brand starts at the beginning of 2019. Our bebo panda team has 3 people, one is a mechanical engineer, one is an acupuncture practitioner and one is a geospatial data analyst. These jobs sound not bad right? Then why we want to start a bubble tea shop? It could be a unhappy workplace, a bad manger, unwell paid salary...

Why we do bubble tea?

Life is fantastic, we don't want to waste our time in a toxic working environment every day. At the same time, all of us love bubble tea and fresh fruit drinks. However, most of drinks or bubble tea containing too much sugar, artificial additives or preservatives. So we start to make bubble tea for ourselves and our friends.

Surprisingly, all of people are happy about our tea. Then why don't we make a fresh, healthy bubble tea and share the superb fresh bubble tea experience with others?

Difficult roads often lead to beautiful destinations. We leave our jobs, we find our way to do the things we like. And we belive you can do it also.

What is your story?

Share your story with us. We gonna collect it ,print it and put it on our story box. Customers wait for a cup of tea, and take away a cup of story. In this big world, there is always someone who can understand you!
